Prefab: Terr Stair Collider
Purpose: The collider to attach to a Terr Stair object to create an incline or decline in terrain
Interaction: Player runs on top of it

How it works:
 - A 2D box collider tilted at 28.5 degrees on the Z plane.
 - Transform has been edited to fit the size of the Terr Stair incline.
 - Attach this collider before you resize the Terr Stair object.
